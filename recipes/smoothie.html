Smoothie

Description

You will find the perfect smoothie is here!

An easy and fast recipe to make one of the most delicious smoothies on Earth, and perhaps other planets too ;-)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1 1/2 cup of milk
  • Ice (optionally)

Steps

  1. Cut the banana in small pieces.
  2. Fill a blender with all the ingredients and blend until smooth. If it's a bit dense, try to use 2 cups of milk instead.
